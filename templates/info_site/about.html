With Dynamine, we aim to help you take full advantage of the hardware you already have to begin mining for cryptocurrencies. With our product, even a less-than tech literate person will be able to set up their machine to make a profit while they are not using it.


What is Cryptocurrency?

▶ An Internet-based medium of exchange distinct from physical (such as banknotes and coins) that exhibits properties similar to physical currencies, but allows for instantaneous transactions and borderless transfer-of-ownership.
▶ A digital currency in which encryption techniques (cryptography) are used to regulate the generation of units of currency and verify the transfer of funds, operating independently of a central bank








What is Cryptocurrency Mining?

▶ To make up for the absence of a centralized banking industry, there must be another way to validate transactions
▶ This is done through a series of mathematical equations that are completed by computer hardware that form the blockchain
▶ Without “miners” to solve these equations, the blockchain would not be realistic. The system creates incentive for miners to do this by rewarding them with the cryptocurrency they are validating

Why is Pooling Important?

▶ Due to the hashing power required to do these complex mathematical equations, it is no longer practical to mine by yourself
▶ This is solved through the pooling of resources together with others mining the same cryptocurrency to increase their chance of completing a block, and therefore increasing their payout
